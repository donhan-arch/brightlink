Shipping Policy

Everything you need to know about how we ship

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Shipping Timeframes

We process and ship all orders as quickly as possible. Below are our standard shipping timeframes:

Standard Shipping — 1-3 Business Days

Most orders ship within 1-3 business days of confirmation.

Order Processing

Orders are processed Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM EST. Orders placed after hours or on weekends will be processed the next business day.

Free Shipping

Free shipping is available on all orders over $350. Orders under $350 may be subject to a shipping fee based on order size and destination.

Where We Ship

We currently ship to all 50 U.S. states. We do not ship internationally at this time.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can use this to monitor your delivery status.

If you have not received tracking information within 3 business days of your order confirmation, please contact us at sales@brightlinksupply.com.

Damaged or Missing Items

If your order arrives damaged or items are missing, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will work quickly to resolve the issue at no cost to you.
